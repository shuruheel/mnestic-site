## LinkedIn

> **mnestic: open source memory for AI agents**
>
> Agents are only as good as what they remember. mnestic is the open source substrate for that memory. It's an embedded graph and vector database, queried in Datalog, that retrieves by meaning, keyword, and relationship in a single call.
>
> It started as a fork of CozoDB, a brilliant engine whose active development stopped in late 2024. We picked it up, made it meaningfully faster (non-blocking vector index builds, roughly 28x faster lookups, one-call hybrid retrieval), and aimed it squarely at agentic memory.
>
> Everything is open and free under MPL-2.0. No closed core, no lock-in. Build on it, fork it, ship it.
>
> Credit for the original design goes to Ziyang Hu and the Cozo Project Authors.
> github.com/shuruheel/mnestic
